What is Faith?

According to nami.org, approximately 1 in 5 adults in the U.S. —43.8 million, or 18.5%— experience mental illness in a given year. Faith was created in order to raise awareness on mental health. Faith allows users to vent, support and spread information based on mental health, illness or any troubles that you be going through. Browse articles that are updated daily - or join the community and help others. We value individuals information so all comments will remain anonymous. Faith is a safe place to express yourself and together we can help each other one by one. We ask that you remain active and continue sharing with the community. Please show respect when replying to others. We thank you for using Faith.
