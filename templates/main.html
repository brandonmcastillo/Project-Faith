What is Faith?

According to nami.org, approximately 1 in 5 adults in the U.S. —43.8 million, or 18.5%— experience mental illness in a given year. Faith was created in order to raise awareness on mental health. Faith allows users to vent, support and spread information based on mental health, illness or any troubles that you or a loved one may be going through. Browse articles that are updated daily - or add your voice and join the community. We value your information so all comments will remain anonymous. Faith is a safe place to express yourself and together we can help each other one by one. We ask that you remain active and continue sharing with the community. Please show respect when replying to others. We thank you for using Faith.

We are here to help

Mental health is not something to be taken lightly. Whether it revolves around depression, anxiety, post traumatic stress, relationships, general health or any other illness, we feel that it's important to communicate with others so that you can recover and discover more about yourself. Our goal is to give you a platform where individuals do not have to be afraid and can comfortably speak up. If you or a loved one are experiencing difficult times, please do not be afraid to seek for professional help outside of Faith.
